AVOCADO TOAST WITH EGG

Serves 2 | prep 5min | cook 5min | ready 10min

INGREDIENTS

  1. 2 Teaspoon butter
  2. 2 Eggs
  3. 1 Ripe avocado, pitted, pilled
  4. 2 Teaspoon lemon juice or to taste
  5. 2 Pinch cayenne pepper
  6. 2 Pinch sea salt to taste
  7. 4 Slices sourdough bread
  8. 2 Pinch ground pepper to taste

DIRECTIONS

  1. Toast the bread until lightly golden.
  2. In a small non-stick pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Crack the eggs into the pan and fry until the whites are set and yolks are still runny, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. In a small bowl, mash the avocado with a fork. Stir in the lemon juice and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Spread the avocado onto the toast. Top with a fried egg and season with cayenne pepper.
